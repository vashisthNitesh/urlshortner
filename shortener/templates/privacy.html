Privacy Policy

We collect minimal analytics data (referrer, device, country, campaign parameters) with IPs hashed. Retention is limited by plan tier and bots are filtered. Cookies support authentication, security, and ad placements for free tiers.

  • Users can request deletion of account data via support.
  • Billing providers manage payment details; we store only customer and subscription identifiers.
  • Geo data is inferred from headers or third-party providers with opt-out for DNT signals.
  • reCAPTCHA protects signups and link creation for free tier.
